    About ProClashGamers
    Meet Jeremy "Sin" Ramos

      Jeremy Ramos aka "SinDragon" aka "Sin" started PcG back in 1996 with a group of friends.
    Jeremy and his wife Jade have been in the gaming scene since a very young age; playing at arcades, internet cafes, lan tornaments, etc. Counter-Strike was the very first game for Pro Clash Gamers.

      "How does anybody find friends? In the traditional community, we search through our pool of neighbors and professional colleagues, of acquaintances and acquaintances of acquaintances, in order to find people who share our values and interests. We exchange information about one another, disclose and discuss our mutual interests, and sometimes we become friends. In a virtual community we can go directly to the place where are favorite subjects are being discussed, then get acquainted with people who share our passions or who use words in a way we find attractive. Your chances of making friends are magnified by orders of magnitude over the old methods of finding a peer group." Come join us at PcG and become family we are a online community that's been around for the past 20 years with over 800 members if you enjoy any of the games tag in this discription please join us.

    Pro Clash Gamers are involved in many different gaming communities consisiting of:
    CS:GO, Valorant, Call of Duty: ModernWarfare, Call of Duty: Black Ops, Rust, and MANY MORE !!
